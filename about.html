Our Story

TriStar Wheel & Tire was created with a passion for trucks, custom wheels, and helping customers build vehicles they are proud to drive. We specialize in premium wheel and tire setups for trucks, off-road builds, show trucks, and daily drivers.

Our goal is simple: provide quality brands, honest recommendations, and personalized service to help every customer find the right setup.
